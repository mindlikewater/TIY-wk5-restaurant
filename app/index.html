Our Story Menu Reservations Games

A group of buddies got together one day and decided that we were tired of our wives not letting us do what we loved in our own home. We developed the concept of taking the "man cave" outside of the home and sharing it with others! Here, anyone can enjoy playing their favorite video games while also tasting original dishes and drinking the best craft beers. Our state of the art sound system ensures a great playing experience. We keep a small collection of regular board games behind the counter, so if you're looking for a different type of gaming experience, we can provide that, as well. Our taps are always rotating, as we continuously sample beers from independent breweries. We try to have at least one localally brewed beer on tap at all times. We know that our brews won't always be familiar. That's why our resident brewmaster, Richard, would be happy to recommend a beer style to suit your taste buds. Don't ever hestitate to ask for a small sample before ordering. We want our customers to be happy with their selections.


For those of you who are still kids at heart, please come visit us at Brews on Board!
